What is VI Help?

VI Help enables visually impaired individuals to navigate the world independently using real-time AI-powered audio feedback.

A wearable camera captures the environment, sends it to a mobile app, and converts it into meaningful voice guidance — instantly.

Our Technology

  • Wearable camera captures real-time video
  • Mobile app processes the feed using AI
  • Instant voice feedback through earpiece
